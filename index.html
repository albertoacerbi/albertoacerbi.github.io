Alberto Acerbi

Lecturer in Psychology, Centre for Culture and Evolution, Brunel University London

I am a cognitive/evolutionary psychologist and anthropologist with a particular interest in computational science, and this is my blog (not much updated lately…) and professional website. I am interested in particular to contemporary cultural phenomena, and I use a naturalistic, quantitative, and evolutionary approach with different methodologies, especially individual-based models and quantitative analysis of large-scale data.

I recently wrote a book for Oxford University Press: Cultural evolution in the digital age, and an open access manual: Individual-based models of cultural evolution. A step-by-step guide using R. You can find here my institutional webpage.
